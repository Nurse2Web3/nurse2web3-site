⚠ Clinical Disclaimer

NoteGen is a documentation and reference aid only. The Note Writer uses AI to expand your shorthand into a draft note; the Diagnosis Lookup provides general clinical education. Every output is a starting point that must be reviewed and validated by the nurse using their own clinical judgment. The AI is instructed never to fabricate clinical facts in notes, but you are solely responsible for the accuracy of anything entered into a patient's chart and for any clinical action taken. NoteGen does not replace nursing knowledge, clinical judgment, physician orders, facility charting standards, care plans, or institutional policy, and must not be used as the sole basis for clinical documentation or care decisions. Do not enter patient identifiers. Nothing you type is stored.
