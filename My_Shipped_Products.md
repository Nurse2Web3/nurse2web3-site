## 5. Rational Investor Decision System™
**Type:** Risk-First Framework · AI-Compatible
**What it does:** A risk-first decision framework for investors and prediction-market participants that evaluates probability, uncertainty, and timing in complex, information-dense environments.

Rather than predicting outcomes or prices, this system focuses on improving decision quality under uncertainty. Built around a structured analytical prompt that guides users to:
- Assess how uncertain an event truly is
- Whether market expectations may be early or overconfident
- What information would meaningfully change the probability of an outcome

The core prompt is designed for use inside ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity. Minimal input required. Applies a disciplined, probability-based lens to prediction markets and broader market/policy decisions.
